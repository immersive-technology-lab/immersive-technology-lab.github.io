Immersive Technology Lab

Institute of Computer Graphics and Vision, TUGraz

Our research is focused on situated visualization for augmented reality, photometric registration for augmented reality rendering, and authoring for augmented/virtual reality.
Situated visualization. Augmented reality technology enables to visualize data in situ for relevant people. Our research interests include on-site light field capturing and visualization.
Photometric registration. Coherent visuals of real and virtual content are essential in augmented reality. Our interests lie in solving this problem with learnable light probes and learned camera components.
Authoring. Extracting data for augmented reality is hard. We address automated tutorial data creation from assembly graph, tab for guitar playing, and 3D scene video.
